Common Questions

How do I create an account?
Visit app.viewvault.app or download the iOS app. Sign in with Google or your email — your account is created automatically.

How do I delete my account?
Email us at support@viewvault.app and we'll remove your account and data within 30 days.

Is ViewVault free?
Yes. ViewVault is free to use with no ads or premium tiers.

Can I self-host ViewVault?
Yes. ViewVault is available as a Docker image for self-hosting. See the GitHub for details.
