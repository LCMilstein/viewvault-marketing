Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 19, 2026

ViewVault ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ViewVault mobile application and web service (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (provided via Google Sign-In or email verification)
  • Display name (from your Google profile, if applicable)
  • Profile picture URL (from your Google profile, if applicable)

Watchlist Data. We store information you provide about media you track, including:

  • Movie and TV show titles, metadata, and watched status
  • Collections and custom lists you create
  • Quality preferences and notes

Usage Data. We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Timestamps of account activity (login times, last active)
  • General usage patterns (e.g., number of items in watchlist)

Information We Do Not Collect. We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, health data, financial information, or browsing history outside the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Sync your watchlist across devices
  • Send transactional communications (e.g., verification codes)
  • Diagnose technical issues and prevent abuse

We may in the future use your information to:

  • Send optional notifications about new features or content recommendations
  • Provide personalized content suggestions based on your watchlist
  • Display relevant advertisements or offer premium subscription features

If we introduce any of these uses, we will update this policy and, where required, obtain your consent.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Auth0 (by Okta) — Authentication and identity management. Auth0 processes your email and login credentials to verify your identity. Auth0 Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — If you choose to sign in with Google, Google shares your name, email, and profile picture with us. Google Privacy Policy
  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — We query TMDB for movie and TV show metadata (titles, posters, descriptions). No personal data is sent to TMDB. TMDB Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — Network security and content delivery. Cloudflare may process your IP address and request metadata. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Apple (App Store) — If you download ViewVault from the App Store, Apple may collect analytics. Apple Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers we operate and is protected using:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS for all connections)
  • Secure credential storage (iOS Keychain for authentication tokens)
  • Hashed passwords (bcrypt) where applicable
  • Network-level protection via Cloudflare

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique credentials for your account.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information and watchlist data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., total number of users) may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your watchlist data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

ViewVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Users

ViewVault is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other regions with data protection laws: we process your data on the legal basis of contractual necessity (to provide the Service you requested) and, where applicable, your consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
